Key Achievements

  • Developed India's first indigenous satellite launch vehicle (SLV-III).
  • Played a key role in the Pokhran-II nuclear tests in 1998.
  • Launched India’s missile program (Agni, Prithvi, BrahMos).
  • Served as the 11th President of India from 2002-2007.
  • Awarded Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honor.
